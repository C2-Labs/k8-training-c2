Training Agenda

This training is designed as an all-day immersion session to become familiar with Kubernetes key concepts and tools. The morning will provide an overview and instructor led demos. The afternoon will allow students to demonstrate their knowledge by deploying and managing applications inside of a Kubernetes cluster.
